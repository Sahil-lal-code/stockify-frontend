About This Project

The Smart Stock Predictor leverages machine learning to analyze historical stock data and forecast future price movements. Our system combines technical indicators with advanced algorithms to generate predictions with measurable accuracy.

Machine Learning Core

Linear Regression: Our baseline model that establishes relationships between historical price features and future values. While simple, it provides a robust foundation for identifying linear trends in market data.

Random Forest: An ensemble method that combines multiple decision trees to capture non-linear patterns and complex interactions in market behavior. This model excels at handling volatility and short-term fluctuations.

Model Evaluation

R² (R-Squared): Measures how well our models explain price variability (0-100%).

For Example --> R² of 0.42 indicates the model captures 42% of price movements - a strong result given market randomness.

MSE (Mean Squared Error): Quantifies prediction accuracy in dollar terms.

For Example --> MSE of 0.112 means predictions are typically within $0.11 of actual prices - competitive for daily forecasting.

Technical Note: All models are regularly retrained using walk-forward validation to adapt to market changes.
